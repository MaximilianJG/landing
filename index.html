How it works

Everyone gets 5 darts

Each Round, each player can throw 5 darts in a row. Keep track of your points each round.

3 rounds each

In total, their are three rounds. (In total 15 shots)

Most points win

At the end each player sums up all of his/her points and the player with the highest score wins
